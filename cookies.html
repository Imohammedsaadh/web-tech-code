Chocolate Chip Cookies

Preparation Time : 10 min

Cook Time : 5 min

Steps To Make A Cookies:

  1. Gather all ingredients: flour, butter, sugar, eggs, baking powder or soda, salt, and extras like vanilla, chocolate chips, or nuts
  2. Cream the butter and sugar together until smooth.
  3. Beat in eggs and vanilla extract.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix the flour, baking powder or soda, and salt.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix the flour, baking powder or soda, and salt.
  6. Add extras (chocolate chips, nuts, etc.) if desired and fold into the dough.
  7. Shape the dough into balls or drop spoonfuls onto a baking tray, leaving space between each cookie.
